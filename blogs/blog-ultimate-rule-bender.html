ULTIMATE RULE BENDERS!! FASHION RULES THAT WE WILL BE BREAKING THIS SEASON

12 August 2021
by Farirai Macheka
  • Edgars
  • Beaty
  • Style

If you think you are a mere mortal when it comes to fashion trends, then think again. Extraordinary summer calls for you to be the ultimate rule bender. After all, rules are meant to be broken. Erase those rules that you have been sticking to and join the summer fun, as we give you a rundown of the rules that we will be kicking to the curb. From beauty to styling your clothes, get you notebook and get ready to take some notes.

Print mixing

The ‘wise’ fashion gods say you can't mix prints. Mixing prints looks messy? Wrong! We have been seeing awesome ways to mix prints and the double print/pattern trend isn't showing signs of waning anytime soon. Whether you love polka dots florals there is nothing so revolutionary like mixing prints. Here is how to master the art of mixing patterns:

  1. The trick is to create a fine balance for the double print trend e.g wearing big polka dots on top and small at the bottom and the reverse also applies
  2. It's in the 'hot mess' that a beautiful aesthetic is created. Embrace clashing prints, wear different prints that are in the same color pallets.
  3. Go for a coordinated color contrast for balanced look and to avoid looking like a hot mess.

Be fashion forward, don't be afraid to play around with you ginghams, florals, geometric patterns, it's not everyone`s cup of tea. Rock the trend with pride. This shows confidence and adds an element of fun to your look.

Sequins are flashy for day

For the longest time you’ve been playing it safe and avoiding rocking clothes with embellishments and those that are super shiny. Prepare to dazzle in sequins during the day without batting an eyelid. Hooray! Your sequins can make it to daytime wear if you break them correctly. Whether it's sequined top, pants or skirts here is how you do it. You don't need to look like a disco lighting ball from head to toe. To tone down the sequins sassiness, pair them with your wardrobe staples -your sequined t-shirt with denim pants, skirts or shorts. Make sure they are not too structured and not too loud if you want to be more subtle and to nail the look.

Big Natural Brows

Check out how we are brow- ing this summer season. Hold on to your eyebrows. It's time to take a style cues from the Queen of swagger Pokello, and your favourite celebrities, because we not plucking out any single hair. Yep you heard us right! Natural big brows are the 'IT' trend this summer season. So no more getting late for meetings threading tweezing, shaping or concealing to get that perfect Insta brow. All you need to do is to clean few extra strands, brush up your brows with a spoolie and you good to go. This trend is here for a long time - all the while thanks to model Cara Delavigne who showed us that natural brows are beautiful. If your brows are sparse, fake it, fill them up with eyebrow gel or powder.

Your social circle will definitely bow to a perfect work of art. So jump on to the band wagon and start leading the pack and unleash the fashionista in you.
