Hospital Cash Plan

EDGARS MY LIFE

Join The Edgars Club, Sign up for the Edgars Hospital Cash Plan and enjoy great benefits!

1. EDGARS HOSPITAL CASH PLAN

  • The Edgars Hospital Cash Plan is not medical aid but a perfect cover against loss of income in the event of a family member being hospitalised for more than 48hrs.
  • A member’s policy will be able to cover a spouse, the children and any other relative they wish to be added to the policy.
  • The Edgars Hospital Cash Plan gives you peace of mind and comfort in times of difficulty due to hospitalisation of either yourself or a family member.
  • At a rate of $500.00 and $2000.00 per day that you are admitted in hospital for an adult and a child respectively, it is a good deal to cover you against loss of income.

2. BENEFITS OF THE EDGARS HOSPITAL CASH BACK PLAN

  • This comes with an ‘inbuilt’ FREE Personal Accident Cover which is twice the Main Funeral Cash Benefit
  • Benefits are starting from a premium of $15.00 for children on the gold plan for a maximum annual benefit of $30,000.00
  • Maximum Main Funeral Cash cover for people aged between 65-75yrs is $6,000.00 (including FREE Personal Accident Cover)
  • FREE Personal Accident Cover is claimable when an insured person has been involved in an accident. 100% is paid when an insured person dies due to an accident. In cases where insured person is injured, up to 20% is payable to service providers when there are Medical Aid shortfalls or when one does not have a Medical Aid.
  • For customers to enjoy the other Edgars Mi-Life benefits, they have to opt for the Main Funeral Cash first.

3. FOR THE ACCOUNT HOLDERS ONLY:

  • $12,000.00 Account credit relief for over 7days in hospital.
  • Death benefit of account balance clearance.
  • NB: Membership is automatic for the account holder upon submission of the sign up forms. All other beneficiaries MUST BE added by the account holder by coming in-store.

4. BIGGEST ADVANTAGES FOR YOU

  • Use the cash in any way you wish to e.g paying school fees, utility bills etc
  • Payouts of $1,250.00 for children and $2,500.00 for adults on the Gold Plan respectively and $2,500.00 for children and $5,000.00 for adults on the Platinum Plan respectively.
  • Add as many dependents as you wish
  • Pre-existing conditions/ailments covered (including HIV/Aids)
  • Should you be diagnosed with HIV/Aids or any chronic ailment after the commencement of your policy, you will be covered
  • Immediate accident cover and three months’ waiting period for illness

5. A plan that covers you and your loved ones on your account now with Edgars Club

  • Please note all EHCP claims MUST be made within 30 days of patient being discharged from the hospital.
  • This is exclusive to club members only. Please call Edgars Club Customer Care line if you are unsure about the product and would like more information.
  • This product is underwritten by Eagle Insurance Company Limited (a member of the FBC Group).
