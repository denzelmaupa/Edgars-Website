THANK U

Earning Loyalty Points

  1. Points will be earned for puchases of all inclusive products.
  2. To earn points at any Edgars and Jet Zimbabwe stores, you must hand your Thank U Rewards Card to the cashier at the till point before you pay for your purchases.
  3. If a Thank U Rewards Card is not presented at the time of purchase, no points will be earned for that purchase. Points lost or forteited due to 'card not present' cannot be claimed retrospectively.
  4. Certain products and channels are exempt from earning points, Edgars and Jet Zimbabwe may amend these products or channels from time to time at their discretion. a. Points can be earned on some of our in-store concession brands, but not on all. To avoid disappointment, please enquire with our sales staff.
  5. Base Thank U Points are earned on all inclusive products at a rate of 10 points per $1.00 spent. This applies to all tender types: cash, account, debit card, credit card and EcoCash.
  6. During any doable points promotions and other events in-store, existing points will not be multiplied and bonus points will be applied only to the base points and on the product.
  7. Bonus points may be earned or awarded at Edgars and Jet Zimbabwe's discretion and campaign criteria and spend thresholds must be satisfied.
  8. Points will be reflected on your Thank U Rewards Account withing 3 (three) working days of your purchase.
  9. Customer points balances will be available in-store at the point-of-sale terminals.
  10. In the event that points can be allocated to you at a later date. Please not that we will be unable to allocated these points at a later date if you do not produce a valid proof of purchase.
  11. Edgars and Jet Zimbabwe reserve the right to change the earning rate and/or value of points at any time, at its discretion. Due notice will be given of any changes.
THANK U

Spending Loyalty Points

  1. Once your points balance reaches 1000 points, you will be able to spend your points by presenting your Thank U Rewards Card and booklet a the till points in participating stores and advising the cashier that you wish to use points as a trade discount against your cash or account purchase. Your points balance will be reduced by the equivalent value spent.
  2. Points can be redeemed on some of our in-store brands, but no all. To avoid disappointment please enquire with our sales staff.
  3. Certain products are exempt from redemption and Edgars and Jet Zimbabwe may amend this list from time to time at their discretion.
  4. Customer points, balances and points redemption information will be available in-store at till points.
  5. Egdars and Jet Zimbabwe reserve the right to change the conversion value of points at any time, at its discretion. Due notice will be given on any changes.
THANK U

Validity of Loyalty Points

  1. Edgars and Jet Zimbabwe reserve the right to purge points on any Thank U Account that has been inactive (i.e. no points earned or redeemed) for a period of 12 (twelve) consecutive months.
  2. All Thank U Points earned but not redeemed within 36 (thirty six) months will be subject to automatic expiry.
  3. Points attached to a lost or damaged card can be transferred to your new card provided they have been spent (due to theft or any other means) prior to reporting the lost/damaged card and obtaining a new card, replacement card. This can be done in-store at the Customer Services counter in participating stores.
  4. Points earned for transactions where goods are later returned will be deducted from your points balance on your Thank U Rewards Account.
  5. Egdars and Jet Zimbabwe, at their sole discretion, may choose to boost your points for a limited period from time to time. Any extra points not used within the time limit will be removed from your balance at the end of the promotion.
  6. Points balances on deliquent or accounts in arrears will not be available for redemption or to spend until such time as the account arrears have been settled in full.
  7. You mau cancel your membership of the programme at any time at any Service counts. When exiting the programme you will forfeit any points already earned but not yet redeemed.
  8. Upon death of a Member, written notification must be provided to Edgars and Jet Zimbabwe, upon receipt of which the account will be closed and the points forfeited. This can be done at the Customer Services counter in-store.
